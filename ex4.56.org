#+title: Exercise 4.56

a. the names of all people who are supervised by Ben Bitdiddle,
together with their addresses;

#+begin_example
  (and (supervisor ?x (Bitdiddle Ben))
       (addresses ?x ?y))
#+end_example

b. all people whose salary is less than Ben Bitdiddle's, together with
their salary and Ben Bitdiddle's salary;

#+begin_example
  (and (salary ?person ?amount)
       (salary (Bitdiddle Ben) ?amount2)
       (list-value < amount amount2))
#+end_example

c. all people who is supervised by someone who is not in the computer
division, together with the supervisor's name and job.

#+begin_example
  (and (supervisor ?person ?sup)
       (not (job ?sup (computer . ?x)))
       (job ?sup ?y))
#+end_example
